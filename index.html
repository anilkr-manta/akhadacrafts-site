About Akhada Crafts

Traditional tools, presented with a premium retail feel.

Akhada Crafts offers handcrafted Indian strength equipment designed around traditional forms. The collection includes clubs for lighter paired work, mudgars in heavier formats, and samtola for classic leverage-based training.

Craft & design

Built around form, grain, balance, and training culture.

Each piece is presented as functional equipment with a strong visual identity. The shapes, profiles, and finishes are intended to reflect traditional Indian training heritage while fitting a refined modern presentation.

  • Handcrafted wooden clubs and mudgars
  • Traditional silhouettes with premium visual presentation
  • Available in lighter club pairs and heavier strength formats
  • Designed for informational browsing and direct inquiry
