Beginner workouts

Starter practice and weight guidance

Simple beginner practice structure based on lighter weights first, then gradual progression.

Workout 1 — Foundation Circles

Recommended weight: 1.1 lb pair clubs

  • Front circles — 2 sets of 20
  • Back circles — 2 sets of 20
  • Pendulum swings — 2 sets of 15
  • Rest 45–60 seconds between sets

Workout 2 — Control & Rhythm

Recommended weight: 1.8–2.2 lb pair clubs

  • Inside circles — 3 sets of 15
  • Outside circles — 3 sets of 15
  • Cross-body swings — 2 sets of 12
  • Short flow rounds — 3 rounds of 45 seconds

Workout 3 — Progression Flow

Recommended weight: 3.3 lb pair clubs

  • Alternating circles — 3 sets of 12
  • Figure-8 pattern — 3 sets of 10
  • Continuous flow practice — 4 rounds of 30–45 seconds
  • Use only after lighter pairs feel fully controlled

Workout 4 — Intro to Mudgar

Recommended weight: 4.4 lb mudgar

  • Single-side swings — 2 sets of 10 each side
  • Shoulder cast pattern — 2 sets of 8
  • Slow, controlled repetitions only
  • Do not start with 11–13.2 lb or heavier as a beginner
