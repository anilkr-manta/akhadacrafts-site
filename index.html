Why train with these tools

What modern training misses

Indian clubs, mugdar, and samtola address movement patterns and joint angles that barbells, dumbbells, and machines simply cannot reach.

01
Full shoulder range of motion
Club swinging uses the full 360° range of the shoulder joint — building strength and flexibility together.
02
Rotator cuff health & prehab
Light club work (500 g–800 g) targets stabiliser muscles with low impact — excellent for injury prevention.
03
Rotational strength & grip
Heavy clubs and the samtola build rotational strength, grip endurance, and core stability.
